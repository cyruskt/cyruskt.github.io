Biography

Hi! My name is Cyrus, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Management at the Trulaske College of Business at the University of University of Missouri.

My research examines how the design of human resource practices both predicts and shapes performance outcomes at the employee and firm levels.

I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Placeholder, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Management. Prior to the Ph.D. program, I gained several years of industry experience in [field].

Outside of research and teaching, I enjoy spending time with family, running, and volunteering in the local community.
